Are you seeing any of these signs?

Most homeowners don’t need a “sales pitch” — they need answers. Check the symptoms you’re noticing and we’ll point you toward the right solution.

White crust on faucets / shower glass

Hard-water scale (limescale) can build up quickly and dull fixtures.

Dry skin / hair after showering

Hard water can feel harsh and make soap and shampoo less effective.

Chlorine taste or “pool” smell

Common with city water treatment — filtration can noticeably improve taste and odor.

Rust stains in sinks / toilets

Often linked to iron or minerals — common on well systems and some municipal lines.

“Rotten egg” smell

Can be sulfur-related (more common on well water) and usually has a targeted fix.

Spotty dishes + dull laundry

Minerals can leave residue and reduce soap performance over time.

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the most common questions homeowners have before getting a whole-home system.

Do I need filtration, softening, or both?
It depends on your water. Filtration targets taste/odor (like chlorine) and certain contaminants. Softening targets hardness minerals that cause scale. Many homeowners benefit from a combo system when both issues are present.
Do you work with city water and well water?
Yes. City water often has chlorine taste/odor and varying hardness. Well water can have hardness, iron staining, sulfur odor, or sediment. The right system depends on the symptoms and local conditions.
How long does installation take?
Most installations are completed in a single visit. We’ll confirm scope and placement before scheduling.
