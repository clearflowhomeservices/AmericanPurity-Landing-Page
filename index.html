Signs you may need whole-home water filtration

If your water has a strong chlorine taste or odor, leaves spots on dishes, stains tubs or sinks, or you notice buildup on faucets and shower heads, a whole-home filtration system can help. The right system can also reduce sediment and improve overall water clarity.

Water softeners vs. filtration

Filtration targets things like chlorine taste/odor and sediment. Softening targets hardness minerals that cause scale and buildup. Many homes benefit from a combo approach — the consultation helps determine what makes the most sense for your goals.
